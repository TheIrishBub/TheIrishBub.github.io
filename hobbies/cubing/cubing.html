Cubing

I am a speedcuber, meaning that I solve twisty puzzles with the intent of completing them as fast as I can. Currently, my PB on the normal Rubik's Cube is 18.29 seconds, set at a competition in Carlisle on September 28th, 2019.

Speaking of competitions, I have been to two of them so far: Clash at Carlisle Fall 2019, on September 28th, 2019, and Pennsylvania 2018, on August 17-19, 2019. You can find my official results on my WCA page here.

I also solve other similar twisty puzzles, such as higher order cubes, like the 4x4 and 7x7, and also puzzles that change shape as you solve them, such as the Square-1 shown on the right.
