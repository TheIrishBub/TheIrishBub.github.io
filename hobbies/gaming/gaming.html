Mario Kart

One of my favorite game series, Mario Kart's Time Trials mode offers players the chance to show their skills by racing to get the best time they can in any of each game's courses.

Here is my Mario Kart 8 Player's Page. Mario Kart 8 is the latest main installment in the series, originally released for the Wii U in May of 2014, but later got a re-release for the Nintendo Switch in April of 2017.
