Music

I like listening to music, whether I'm walking somewhere, doing schoolwork, or just chilling out. While I will listen to just about anything, I prefer the metal genre, with my favorite bands being DragonForce, Disturbed, and Metallica.

I also play instruments. When I was in elementary school, I played the violin and sang in the chorus, the latter of which I carried into middle school. Then when I started high school, I learned to play the guitar, before finally switching to bass guitar.

I am a part of a music club at Thaddeus Stevens, where we play music and jam out!

I also find that I tap my feet and hands to whatever beat is in my head. That being said, I do not play drums. I've tried, and I lack the coordination to be able to move my limbs independently of one another.
