I don't have too much to show here, as I only worked on one project, but I want it talk about it here anyway. First, a bit of back story: one of my favorite games of all time is Golden Sun: The Lost Age. It was a game I played a lot of when I was a kid. Some late-game items are difficult to obtain, and are made easier with RNG manipulation methods (RNG stands for Random Number Generator). The RNG in this game follows a specific formula, and when one knows the formula and what advances it, one can predict the outcome of events in the game.

I have written a program in C++ that will give me a list of random numbers, and I can then use those numbers to determine what events will happen and when they will happen.

At a later point, I would like to add the program to this site, but as of right now, I don't yet have the knowledge to be able to do that.
